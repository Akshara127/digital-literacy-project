# Social Media Do's and Don'ts for College Students

## DO'S

1. Keep your profile professional - use a decent profile 
   picture and appropriate bio.
2. Think before you post - once online, content stays forever.
3. Use strong, unique passwords for every social media account.
4. Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) on all accounts.
5. Report cyberbullying or harassment immediately 
   to the platform.

## DON'TS

1. Don't share your OTP, password, or personal details 
   with anyone online.
2. Don't post your location, address, or daily routine publicly.
3. Don't engage with strangers who send suspicious links 
   or friend requests.
4. Don't post offensive or inappropriate content - it can 
   affect your academic and professional future.
5. Don't click on unknown links or ads claiming prizes 
   or free offers.
