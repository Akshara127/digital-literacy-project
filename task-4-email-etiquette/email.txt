Email Etiquette

1. Use a clear subject line.
2. Start with a greeting.
3. Be polite and professional.
4. Keep the message short and clear.
5. Avoid spelling mistakes.
6. End with a proper closing.

Example Email:

Subject: Assignment Submission

Dear Sir/Ma’am,

I hope you are doing well.
I am submitting my assignment for your review.

Thank you.

Regards,
Akshara Khandelwal
