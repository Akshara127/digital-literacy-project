Cybercrime

Cybercrime refers to illegal activities done using computers or the internet.

Types of Cybercrime:
1. Phishing – Fake emails to steal information
2. Hacking – Unauthorized access to systems
3. Identity Theft – Stealing personal data
4. Online Fraud – Scams and fake offers

Prevention:
1. Use strong passwords
2. Do not share personal information
3. Avoid suspicious links
4. Use antivirus software
