STAY SAFE ONLINE - PREVENTION CHECKLIST
For College Students in India

1. Never click on suspicious links received via email, 
   WhatsApp, or SMS - even if they look official.

2. Always check the website URL before entering any 
   login details - fake sites have slightly different URLs.

3. Never share your OTP with anyone - not even bank 
   employees or customer care.

4. Never share your UPI PIN or bank account details 
   on any call or message - no bank ever asks for this.

5. Use strong passwords - mix of uppercase, lowercase, 
   numbers and symbols. Change them every 3 months.

6. Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) on all 
   important accounts like email, Instagram, and banking apps.

7. Do not use public WiFi for UPI payments or 
   net banking transactions.

8. Regularly check your bank statements and UPI 
   transaction history for any unknown transactions.

9. Keep your mobile and apps updated - updates fix 
   security vulnerabilities.

10. If you receive a suspicious call asking for money 
    or personal details, disconnect immediately and 
    report it.

REPORTING CHANNELS:
- National Cyber Crime Portal: cybercrime.gov.in
- Cyber Crime Helpline: 1930 (24x7)
